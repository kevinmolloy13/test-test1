Perimenopause & Menopause
Symptom Checker

Use this as a starting point to understand what you might be experiencing — and take it to your GP appointment.

There are over 40 recognised symptoms of perimenopause and menopause. No two journeys are the same. This checklist can help you identify what you're experiencing and start the conversation with your doctor.

🌡 Vasomotor

  • Hot flushes / hot flashes
  • Night sweats
  • Facial flushing
  • Chills after hot flush

🌙 Sleep

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Waking during the night
  • Early morning waking
  • Fatigue / exhaustion

💕 Mood & Mental Health

  • Anxiety / feeling on edge
  • Low mood or depression
  • Irritability / mood swings
  • Loss of confidence
  • Tearfulness

🧠 Brain & Cognitive

  • Brain fog
  • Memory lapses
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Word-finding difficulties

💪 Physical

  • Joint aches & pains
  • Headaches / migraines
  • Heart palpitations
  • Weight changes
  • Bloating / dizziness

✨ Skin & Hair

  • Dry / itchy skin
  • Skin crawling or tingling
  • Hair thinning or loss
  • Brittle nails

The full checklist includes urogenital health, sexual health, menstrual changes and more — plus a notes section for your GP visit.
