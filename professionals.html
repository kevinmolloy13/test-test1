Fertility & Hormonal Health

Dr. Codruța — Saol Nua Clinic

I'm delighted to have Dr. Codruța as part of this trusted community. Dr. Codruța is a Dublin-based Restorative Reproductive Medicine doctor and founder of Saol Nua Clinic, with over 17 years of clinical experience helping individuals and couples navigate fertility and hormonal health challenges. Her expertise includes fertility optimisation, recurrent miscarriage support, PCOS, endometriosis, IVF support, and integrative reproductive medicine.

Three women, drawn together by their shared experience and journey. Nutrition & Menopause Support

Norma Psillos — Empowered Health

Norma Psillos is a Registered Nutritional Therapist and Accredited Menopause Educator, supporting women through perimenopause, menopause and beyond. Using an evidence-based and compassionate approach, she helps women understand the root causes of their symptoms through personalised nutrition and lifestyle strategies.

Norma also supports women with PCOS, endometriosis, irregular or heavy periods, and POI. She works one-to-one from her Limerick clinic, online via Zoom, and delivers educational talks and workplace workshops, empowering women to feel informed, confident and back in control of their health.
