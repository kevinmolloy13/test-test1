Fertility & Hormonal Health

Dr. Codruța — Saol Nua Clinic

I'm delighted to have Dr. Codruța as part of this trusted community. Dr. Codruța is a Dublin-based Restorative Reproductive Medicine doctor and founder of Saol Nua Clinic, with over 17 years of clinical experience helping individuals and couples navigate fertility and hormonal health challenges. Three women, drawn together by their shared experience and journey.
