The Ladies Let's Talk
Tracker & Journal

6-Month Journal Durable Hardback Lay-Flat Binding Ribbon Divider GP Visit Section Daily Tracking

Navigate your perimenopause and menopause journey with the Ladies Let's Talk Tracker & Journal — a gentle, supportive six-month journal designed to help you feel more in tune with your body.

With space to track daily changes in hormones, mood, sleep, and physical symptoms, it helps you notice patterns, understand what's happening, and feel a little more in control each day.

There's also a dedicated GP visit section, giving you a place to write down questions, concerns, and anything you don't want to forget — so you can walk into every appointment feeling prepared and confident.

Because this stage of life deserves understanding, support, and a space that's just for you.
