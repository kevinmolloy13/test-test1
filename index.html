About the founder

Hi, I'm Jennifer

I created Ladies Let's Talk Collective because I, like many women, found myself searching for answers — trying to understand the changes happening in my body and not always knowing where to turn.

I'm not a medical professional, but I believe every woman deserves access to honest conversations and trusted information. That's why I connect with experts and bring those conversations to you — so you can feel more informed, more supported, and less alone.
